My Market Outlook: July 2024

July 9, 2024

the equity market looks pretty insane right now.

NVIDIA becomes the largest company by MC then loses almost a trillion $ in a few days,
Tesla slightly beats delivery estimates (still down YOY), then shoots up $80 per share a week.
Meta loses 10% when it beats estimates because Zuck talked about the Metaverse again,
but then goes up 6% because he posted himself wakeboarding in a tux.
and obviously meme stocks and crypto, which need no explanation

i think there are only two broad future scenarios:

  • AI makes humanity unnecessary but the economy very efficient, so the market shoots up exponentially
  • the national debt problem becomes too much of a problem, causing inflation and a continuing bull market, but ultimatly a horrible crash and possibly war

    • a part of me thinks the second option is most likely, and the hype around AI is because the promise of efficiency would solve the debt problem.

    and i don't think LLMs will create AGI. Maybe i'm just hoping there is more to intelligence than creating statistical models based on empirical data

    and if AGI is unleashed, the incumbants will take the most advantage and there is less of a chance for young people to build their own empires

    it is an unfortunate thought that throughout history, fallen empires were usually replaced by more advanced and virtuous ones

    but having technology as our ruler would feel disconnected. all people would be commoditized if intelligence were no object

    i think that the socialist values will be taken to their natural conclusion in my lifetime

    there will be advancements made in longevity, technological (cowardly) warfare, and all of our current actions will be replaced by technology

    competition will be illusory and optional, and comfort will always be available.

    its unfortunate that this will happen in my lifetime, for what is really great about this is that when it falls, it's opposite will prevail

    and the tragedy of the human condition - with it's pain, disappointment, early death, and all its sudden losses - will be embraced,
    because we know the consequences of neglecting human autonomy in attempt to solve this tragedy.

    matthew mueller 2024
