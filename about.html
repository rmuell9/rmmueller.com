home
     

i am not a stem person and am not super advanced in anything technical. i studied finance at college and am learning how to program after reading paul graham and learning about quants.

i read a lot, and am currently focusing on writers like taleb, mandelbrot, michael lewis, pascal, natenborg, montaine and o reilly python books.

it is obvious to me that i am highly influnced by talebs incerto series - it has basically taken over my intellectual life.

but i learned about his work through naval, who i learned about through joe rogan, who i learned about from the youtube algorithm.

but what kept me reading taleb was that he reminded me of nietzsche, who i think is the best thinker that has ever lived (or at least wrote)

i learned about nietzsche from carl jung, who i learned about from jordan peterson, which is embarassing considering how peterson is an idiot.

anyways, if i were to die today, i would want something of me that is written down. i dont want to be remembered only through the words of other people.

i try not to think about how i am percieved and what "role" people see me in

because when i do, i lose myself in the sense that i am constrainied to an image. and the original image get morphed into the image people want me to be.

and that is how i wish to be remembered, someone who just followed their intrisic inclinations. and that is enough for my life

and i think ive always been this way, and because of it i think i had an unhappy childhood and especially teenage years

i cannot even describe myself, but i would say i am a mix between the secluded intellectual and the reckless risk taker.

this to me is the ideal mix of personality, as one side is reflective and adaptive, while the other is empircal and results oriented

but the consequence is that i am alone.

there is nobody to understand my internal life and my intellectual passions, and nobody who is willing to be with me during the risk taking

even so i am not the most extreme risk taker, i am an amateur

so if i were to die today, i would want to be remembered in terms of the books i read, my passion for combat sports, and my drive to be great

and what makes me feel unsatisfied is that these are all evident of potential, not kinetic, achievement.

so i have done nothing so far. and that is painful.
